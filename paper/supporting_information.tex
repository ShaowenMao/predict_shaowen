\section*{Introduction}

This Supporting Information provides methodological and diagnostic detail that supports the main manuscript but is not required to follow its principal conclusions. It documents the PREDICT ensemble design, the construction of within-window state libraries and cross-window permeability cases, and the replay and multiphase-upscaling checks used to prepare reservoir-model inputs. Key analysis and interpretation remain in the main manuscript. Data and software are deposited separately and cited through the manuscript's Open Research Statement.
